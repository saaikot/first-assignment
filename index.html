Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in my ticket?

Every ticket covers full access to talks for your plan's day range, meals during conference hours, and a digital badge for networking. Workshop and recording access depend on the tier — check the pricing table above.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket?

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event, and transferable to a colleague any time before check-in — just email us with the new attendee's details.

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

Yes, for workshop and hands-on sessions. Talks and panels don't require one. Power strips and Wi-Fi are provided throughout the venue.

Will sessions be recorded?

All main-stage talks are recorded. Standard tickets include 30 days of digital access; Pro and Team tickets include lifetime access to the full library.

Is the venue accessible?

Yes — step-free entrances, accessible restrooms, and reserved seating are available on every floor. Let us know your requirements when you register and we'll take care of the rest.
